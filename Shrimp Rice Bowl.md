# Shrimp Rice Bowl
Adapted from http://lovelylittlekitchen.com/cilantro-lime-rice-shrimp-bowl/

## Ingredients

**Shrimp** 
- Cumin
- Garlic finely chopped
- Paprika
- Cayenne pepper
- Shrimp
- Lime

**Cilantro Lime Rice**
- 2 tablespoon butter
- 1/4 c chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed through a garlic press
- jalapeño diced
- 2c white rice 
- 2 cups broth
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- lime zest
- 1/4 tsp Sea Salt
- 4 tbsp chopped cilantro

## Prep 

### Rice

1. In a medium pan melt butter 
2. sauté 
    - onion
    - garlic 
    - jalapeños
3. Add and boil
    - rice
    - broth 
    - lime juice
    - salt
    - 2 tbsp Cilntro
4. When boilding reduce heat   
5. Add 
    - 1 tbsp butter 
    - rest of cilantro (2tbsp)
    - lime zest 

### Shrimp
1. Melt butter in skillet
2. Whisk spices into butter
3. Cook until shrimp done and pink

## Serve 
Shrimp over warm rice, with cilantro and lime
